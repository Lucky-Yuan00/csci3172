About - Eight Great Cuisines

This app explores Chinese culinary traditions through the lens of the “Eight Great Cuisines” (Ba Cai): Shandong (Lu), Cantonese (Yue), Fujian (Min), Jiangsu (Su), Zhejiang (Zhe), Hunan (Xiang), Anhui (Hui), and Sichuan (Chuan). Each cuisine reflects regional ingredients, climate, and craft—from the fresh, delicate notes of Jiangsu and Zhejiang, to the smoky heat of Hunan and the numbing “mala” of Sichuan; from Shandong's masterful braises to Cantonese dim sum and gentle steaming.

Our primary focus is Chinese cuisine. To broaden discovery, search results may occasionally include a small number of non-Chinese recipes as well. We're continuously adding more dishes and improving coverage.
